





SUMMARY









This enactment amends the Patent Act to repeal the power of the 
Governor in Council to make regulations preventing the infringement 
of a patent by any person who makes, constructs, uses or sells a patented 
invention solely for uses reasonably related to the development and 
submission of information required under any law of Canada, a 
province or a country other than Canada that regulates the manufacture, 
construction, use or sale of any product.
