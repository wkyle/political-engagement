





SUMMARY









The purpose of this enactment is to enable the Minister of Transport 
to sell the shares of VIA Rail.










It transfers the existing shares into the name of the Minister in trust 
for the Crown. It also allows the company to issue other shares. Sale of 
all shares must be on terms satisfactory to the Minister of Transport and 
the Minister of Finance.










It also authorizes the Minister to enter into agreements to sell the 
existing or later issued shares of the company and sell or settle its debts 
or obligations.
