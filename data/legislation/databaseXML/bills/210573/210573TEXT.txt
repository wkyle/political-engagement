





1st Session, 37th Parliament,
49-50-51 Elizabeth II, 2001-2002 










House of Commons of Canada










BILL C-433









An Act to amend the Criminal Code (puppy 
mills)







R.S., c. C-46


Her Majesty, by and with the advice and 
consent of the Senate and House of Commons 
of Canada, enacts as follows:









1. The Criminal Code is amended by 
adding the following after section 182.3:







Aggravating 
circumstances 
for sentencing 
purposes


182.31 Without limiting the generality of 
section 718.2, where a court imposes a 
sentence on a person under subsection 
182.2(2) or 182.3(3), the ownership of a puppy 
mill by that person or the participation by that 
person in the operation of a puppy mill shall be 
deemed to be aggravating circumstances 
relating to the offence that the court shall 
consider under paragraph 718.2(a).  










2. This Act comes into force on the day on 
which section 8 of Bill C-15B, introduced in 
the 1st session of the 37th Parliament and 
entitled An Act to amend the Criminal Code 
(cruelty to animals and firearms) and the 
Firearms Act, comes into force.
