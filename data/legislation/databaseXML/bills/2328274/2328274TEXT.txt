CHAPTER 36









An Act to amend the Canada Petroleum 
Resources Act










[Assented to 24th November, 1994]

Her Majesty, by and with the advice and 
consent of the Senate and House of Commons 
of Canada, enacts as follows:









1. Subsection 114(5) of the Canada 
Petroleum Resources Act is replaced by the 
following:







Idem


(5) Notwithstanding any other provision of 
this Act, the Norman Wells Agreement of 
1944 and the Norman Wells Expansion 
Agreement of 1983 shall continue in force in 
accordance with the terms and conditions of 
those Agreements, as amended by the Norman 
Wells Amending Agreement of 1994, and 
sections 1 to 117 do not apply to any of those 
Agreements.
