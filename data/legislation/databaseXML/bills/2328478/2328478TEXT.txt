






1nd Session, 35th Parliament,
42-43-44 Elizabeth II, 1994-95










The House of Commons of Canada










BILL C-300









An Act to amend the Broadcasting Act 
(broadcasting policy)







1991, c. 11; 
1993, c. 38; 
1994, cc. 18, 
26


Her Majesty, by and with the advice and 
consent of the Senate and House of Commons 
of Canada, enacts as follows:









1. Paragraph 3(1)(t) of the Broadcasting 
Act is amended by adding the following 
after subparagraph (ii):










(ii.1) should not demand or receive 
payment for the provision or sale of a new 
programming service without the express 
prior consent of the purchaser or 
potential purchaser of the service, and









(ii.2) should not use a marketing 
technique to promote a new 
programming service in which payment 
is demanded or received from a person to 
whom they are providing or have 
promised to provide a programming 
service where the person has not 
consented to purchase the new service,
