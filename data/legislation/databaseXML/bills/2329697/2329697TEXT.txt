





2nd Session, 35th Parliament,
45 Elizabeth II, 1996-97










The House of Commons of Canada










BILL C-236









An Act to prevent the importation of 
radioactive waste into Canada










Her Majesty, by and with the advice and 
consent of the Senate and House of Commons 
of Canada, enacts as follows:






Short title


1. This Act may be cited as the Radioactive 
Waste Importation Act.







Definitions


2. In this Act,







``Board''
« Commission
 »


``Board'' means the Atomic Energy Control 
Board established pursuant to section 3 of 
the Atomic Energy Control Act;







``radioactive 
waste''
« déchets 
radioactifs »


``radioactive waste'' means material that falls 
within the definition prescribed for 
radioactive waste by the Board.







No import of 
radioactive 
waste


3. No person shall import radioactive waste 
into Canada.







Her Majesty 
bound


4. Her Majesty in right of Canada or a 
province is bound by this Act.
