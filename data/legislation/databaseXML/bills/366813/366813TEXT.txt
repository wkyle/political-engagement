





1st Session, 37th Parliament,
49-50-51 Elizabeth II, 2001-2002 










House of Commons of Canada










BILL C-463









An Act to amend the Employment Insurance 
Act (arm's length dealing)







1996, c. 23


Her Majesty, by and with the advice and 
consent of the Senate and House of Commons 
of Canada, enacts as follows:









1. (1) Paragraphs 5(2)(g) to (i) of the 
Employment Insurance Act are replaced by 
the following:










(g) employment that constitutes an 
exchange of work or services; and  









(h) employment excluded by regulations 
made under subsection (6).









(2) Subsection 5(3) of the Act is repealed.
