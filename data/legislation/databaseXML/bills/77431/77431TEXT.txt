





1st Session, 37th Parliament,
49-50 Elizabeth II, 2001 










House of Commons of Canada










BILL C-404









An Act to amend the Canada Post Corporation 
Act (mail contractors)







R.S., c. C-10


Her Majesty, by and with the advice and 
consent of the Senate and House of Commons 
of Canada, enacts as follows:









1. Subsection 13(5) of the Canada Post 
Corporation Act is replaced by the 
following:







Canada 
Labour Code


(5) Notwithstanding any provision of Part I 
of the Canada Labour Code, for the purposes 
of the application of that Part to the 
Corporation and to officers and employees of 
the Corporation, a mail contractor, except a 
rural route contractor and a suburban service 
contractor,  is deemed not to be a dependent 
contractor or an employee within the meaning 
of those terms in subsection 3(1) of that Act.
