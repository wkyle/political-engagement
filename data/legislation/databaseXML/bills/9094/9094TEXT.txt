An Act to amend the Indian Act (obligations 
of landlords and tenants on reserve land)

(2nd Supp.),

Her Majesty, by and with the advice and 
consent of the Senate and House of Commons 
of Canada, enacts as follows:









1. The Indian Act is amended by adding 
the following after section 88:







Residential 
leases


88.1 Notwithstanding this Act, provincial 
laws relating to the rights and obligations of 
landlords and tenants apply to a lease for 
residential purposes of land or a dwelling unit 
on land in a reserve that is granted










(a) by an Indian or any other person acting 
as sub-lessor under a lease from the 
Minister under subsection 58(3); or









(b) by the Minister acting as lessor under 
subsection 58(3).
