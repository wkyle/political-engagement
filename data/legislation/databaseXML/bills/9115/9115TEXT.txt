





1st Session, 36th Parliament,
46-47 Elizabeth II, 1997-98










The House of Commons of Canada










BILL C-416









An Act to amend the Corrections and 
Conditional Release Act (accelerated 
parole reviews)







1992, c. 20; 
1993, c. 34; 
1995, cc. 22, 
39, 42; 1996, 
c. 19;

Her Majesty, by and with the advice and 
consent of the Senate and House of Commons 
of Canada, enacts as follows:









1. Subparagraph 125(1)(a)(iv) of the 
Corrections and Conditional Release Act is 
replaced by the following:










(iv) an offence set out in Schedule II, or









(v) an offence under section 463 of the 
Criminal Code relating to an offence set 
out in paragraph (1)(e), (2)(f) or (3)(e) of 
Schedule II that was prosecuted by way 
of indictment;
