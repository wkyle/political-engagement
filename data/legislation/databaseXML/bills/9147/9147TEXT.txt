





1st Session, 36th Parliament,
46-47-48 Elizabeth II, 1997-98-99










The House of Commons of Canada










BILL C-303









An Act to amend the Expropriation Act 
(disposal of expropriated lands)







R.S., c. E-21; 
R.S., c. 20 
(2nd Supp.); 
1994, c. 43; 
1996, cc. 10, 
16


Her Majesty, by and with the advice and 
consent of the Senate and House of Commons 
of Canada, enacts as follows:









1. The Expropriation Act is amended by 
adding the following after section 21:







Disposal of 
expropriated 
lands


21.1 Notwithstanding any other Act of 
Parliament, the Crown shall not dispose of 
land that it has expropriated under this Act 
without giving the owner from whom the land 
was taken the first chance to repurchase the 
land on the terms of the best offer received by 
the Crown.
