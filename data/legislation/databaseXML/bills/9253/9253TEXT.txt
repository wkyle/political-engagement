Her Majesty, by and with the advice and 
consent of the Senate and House of Commons 
of Canada, enacts as follows:









1. Section 1 of Part II of Schedule III to 
the Excise Tax Act is repealed.










2. Section 1 of Part VIII of Schedule VI to 
the Act is repealed.
