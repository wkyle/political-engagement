Her Majesty, by and with the advice and 
consent of the Senate and House of Commons 
of Canada, enacts as follows:









1. The Canada Labour Code is amended 
by adding the following after section 4:







Members of 
the Royal 
Canadian 
Mounted 
Police who 
are peace 
officers


4.1 This Part applies in respect of members 
of the Royal Canadian Mounted Police who 
are peace officers pursuant to the Royal 
Canadian Mounted Police Act.  










2. Section 6 of the Act is replaced by the 
following:







Employees of 
Her Majesty


6. Except as provided by sections 4.1 and  5, 
this Part does not apply in respect of 
employment by Her Majesty in right of 
Canada.
