





1st Session, 36th Parliament,
46-47-48 Elizabeth II, 1997-98-99










The House of Commons of Canada










BILL C-487









An Act respecting a National Day Against 
Impaired Driving







Preamble


WHEREAS impaired driving causes 
numerous fatalities every year;










 AND WHEREAS it is important to 
continue to make Canadians aware of the 
dangers and consequences of impaired 
driving;










NOW THEREFORE, Her Majesty, by and 
with the advice and consent of the Senate and 
House of Commons of Canada, enacts as 
follows:










 









SHORT TITLE






Short title


1. This Act may be cited as the National 
Day Against Impaired Driving Act.










NATIONAL DAY AGAINST IMPAIRED DRIVING






National Day 
Against 
Impaired 
Driving


2. Throughout Canada, in each and every 
year, the 14th of August shall be known under 
the name of ``National Day Against Impaired 
Driving''.
