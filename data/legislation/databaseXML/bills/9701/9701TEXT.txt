Her Majesty, by and with the advice and 
consent of the Senate and House of Commons 
of Canada, enacts as follows:









SHORT TITLE









1. This Act may be cited as the 
Homeowners' Freedom from Double 
Taxation Act.










2. The Income Tax Act is amended by 
adding the following after paragraph 
60(m.1):







Property taxes 
paid in respect 
of a principal 
residence


(m.2) where the taxpayer is an individual, 
the property taxes paid to a province or a 
Canadian municipality by the taxpayer in 
respect of property that is the taxpayer's 
principal residence and is owned by the 
taxpayer.
