





2nd Session, 36th Parliament,
48-49 Elizabeth II, 1999-2000










The House of Commons of Canada










BILL C-452









An Act to amend the Motor Vehicle Safety Act 
(ignition security switches)







1993, c. 16


Her Majesty, by and with the advice and 
consent of the Senate and House of Commons 
of Canada, enacts as follows:









1. Subsection 5(1) of the Motor Vehicle 
Safety Act is amended by adding the 
following after paragraph (a):










(a.1) the vehicle is equipped with a device 
that









(i) prevents the engine from being started 
unless an ignition key has been inserted 
in the ignition switch, and









(ii) conforms to the prescribed stan
dards;  









2. Paragraph 5(1)(b) of the Act is 
replaced by the following:










(b) evidence of conformity with the 
standards referred to in paragraphs (a) and 
(a.1)  has been obtained and produced in the 
prescribed form and manner or, where the 
regulations so provide, in a form and 
manner satisfactory to the Minister;









3. This Act comes into force two years 
after the day upon which it receives royal 
assent.
