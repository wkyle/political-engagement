1. Subsection 118.2(2) of the Income Tax 
Act is amended by adding the following 
after paragraph (n):










(n.1) for any herbal remedy that is









(i) purchased for use by a patient who has 
been certified by a medical practitioner 
to have a severe allergy or a severe 
environmental sensitivity to a drug, 
medicament, preparation or substance 
described in paragraph (n), and









(ii) prescribed by a medical practitioner 
as a substitute for that drug, medicament, 
preparation or substance.

222
