





1st Session, 37th Parliament,
49-50 Elizabeth II, 










House of Commons of Canada










BILL C-248









An Act to Amend the Competition Act







R.S., 1985, c. 
C-34


Her Majesty, by and with the advice and 
consent of the Senate and House of Commons 
of Canada, enacts as follows:






added, R.S., c. 
19 (2nd 
Supp.), s. 45


1. Section 96 of the Competition Act is 
amended by adding the following after 
subsection (3):







Interpreta-
tion


(4) For the purpose of subsection (1), gains 
in efficiency cannot offset the effects of a 
lessening or prevention of competition unless 
the majority of the benefits derived or to be 
derived from such gains in efficiency are 
being or are likely to be passed on to 
customers within a reasonable time in the 
form of lower prices.







Non-applica-
tion of section


(5) This section does not apply where, after 
the transaction has been completed, the merg
er or proposed merger, will result or is likely 
to result in the creation or strengthening of a 
dominant market position.
