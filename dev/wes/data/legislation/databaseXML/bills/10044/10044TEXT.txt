





1st Session, 37th Parliament,
49-50 Elizabeth II, 2001 










House of Commons of Canada










BILL C-260









An Act to amend the Holidays Act (National 
Heritage Day) and to make consequential 
amendments to other Acts







R.S., 1985, c. 
H-5


Her Majesty, by and with the advice and 
consent of the Senate and House of Commons 
of Canada, enacts as follows:









1. The Holidays Act is amended by adding 
the following after section 4:










NATIONAL HERITAGE DAY






National 
Heritage Day


5. February 21 is a legal holiday and shall be 
kept and observed as such throughout Canada 
under the name of ``National Heritage Day''.  










CONSEQUENTIAL AMENDMENTS






R.S., 1985, c. 
B-4

(i) Sundays, New Year's Day, National

(2) Except as otherwise provided by this 
Division, when New Year's Day, National 
Heritage Day, Canada Day, Remembrance
