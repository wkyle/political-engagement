





1st Session, 37th Parliament,
49-50 Elizabeth II, 2001 










House of Commons of Canada










BILL C-330









An Act to Amend the Criminal Code 
(desecration of the Canadian flag)







R.S., c. C-46


Her Majesty, by and with the advice and 
consent of the Senate and House of Commons 
of Canada, enacts as follows:









1. The Criminal Code is amended by 
adding the following after section 56:







Desecration of 
flag


56.1 (1) Every one who, without lawful 
excuse, wilfully burns, defaces, defiles, 
mutilates, tramples upon or otherwise 
desecrates the national flag of Canada is guilty 
of an offence and liable on summary 
conviction










(a) for a first offence, to a fine of $500; and









(b) for a second or subsequent offence, to a 
fine of a minimum of $500 and a maximum 
of $15,000.






Exception


(2) No person is guilty of an offence under 
subsection (1) if the person disposes of the 
national flag of Canada because the flag has 
become worn, soiled or damaged.
