





1st Session, 37th Parliament,
49-50 Elizabeth II, 2001 










House of Commons of Canada










BILL C-374









An Act to establish Merchant Navy Veterans 
Day







Preamble


Whereas the veterans of the Merchant 
Navy have served honourably and 
courageously in the defence of Canada 
throughout the years;










 And Whereas the House of Commons 
wishes to set aside a day to acknowledge the 
contribution of the veterans of the Merchant 
Navy to ensuring the freedom and democracy 
enjoyed by Canadians;










Now, Therefore, Her Majesty, by and with the 
advice and consent of the Senate and House of 
Commons of Canada, enacts as follows:










 









SHORT TITLE






Short title


1. This Act may be cited as the Merchant 
Navy Veterans Day Act.










MERCHANT NAVY VETERANS DAY






Merchant 
Navy Veterans 
Day


2. Throughout Canada, in each and every 
year, the third day of September shall be 
known as ``Merchant Navy Veterans Day''.
