






42-43-44 ELIZABETH II









CHAPTER 47









An Act to amend the National Housing Act










[Assented to 15th December, 1995] 







R.S., c. N-11; 
R.S., c. 8 (1st 
Supp.), c.20 
(2nd Supp.),

(4th Supp.);

Her Majesty, by and with the advice and 
consent of the Senate and House of Commons 
of Canada, enacts as follows:






1992, c. 32, s. 
25


1. Section 21 of the National Housing Act 
is replaced by the following:







Aggregate 
maximum


21. Notwithstanding anything in this Act, 
the aggregate outstanding amount of all 
insured loans may not exceed the sum of










(a) one hundred and fifty billion dollars, 
and









(b) any additional amounts authorized by 
Parliament for the purposes of this section 
under an appropriation Act or any other Act 
of Parliament.






Application


2. This Act applies in respect of the 1994 
calendar year and each subsequent 
calendar year.
