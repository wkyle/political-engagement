






42-43-44 ELIZABETH II









CHAPTER 13









An Act to amend the Farm Improvement and 
Marketing Cooperatives Loans Act










[Assented to 15th June, 1995] 







R.S., c. 25 
(3rd Supp.); 
1991, c. 47; 
1992, c. 1; 
1994, c. 38


Her Majesty, by and with the advice and 
consent of the Senate and House of Commons 
of Canada, enacts as follows:









1. Section 7 of the Farm Improvement and 
Marketing Cooperatives Loans Act is re
placed by the following:







Aggregate 
limit


7. The Minister is not liable under this Act 
to make any payment to a lender in respect of 
loss sustained by it as a result of a loan made 
by it during a fiscal year after the aggregate 
principal amount of the loans under this Act 
and of the guaranteed farm improvement 
loans under the Farm Improvement Loans Act 
made during that fiscal year and the four 
preceding fiscal years by all lenders exceeds 
three billion dollars.
