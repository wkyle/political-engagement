





1st Session, 37th Parliament,
49-50-51 Elizabeth II, 2001-2002 










House of Commons of Canada










BILL C-447









An Act to amend the Income Tax Act







R.S., c. 1 (5th 
Supp.)


Her Majesty, by and with the advice and 
consent of the Senate and House of Commons 
of Canada, enacts as follows:









1. Subsection 18(1) of the Income Tax Act 
is amended by striking out the word ``and'' 
at the end of paragraph (u), by adding the 
word ``and'' at the end of paragraph (v) and 
by adding the following after paragraph 
(v):







Fine or 
penalty


(w) any fine or penalty imposed by law on 
the taxpayer in connection with the 
operation of the business.
