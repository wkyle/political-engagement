





2nd Session, 37th Parliament,
51 Elizabeth II, 2002 










House of Commons of Canada










BILL C-244









An Act requiring the national flag of Canada 
to be flown at half-mast on 
Remembrance Day










Her Majesty, by and with the advice and 
consent of the Senate and House of Commons 
of Canada, enacts as follows:









SHORT TITLE






Short title


1. This Act may be cited as the 
Remembrance Day National Flag Act.










INTERPRETATION






Definition of 
``federal 
premises''


2. In this Act, ``federal premises'' means 
land or buildings owned or occupied by Her 
Majesty in right of Canada.










NATIONAL FLAG OF CANADA






National flag 
of Canada at 
half-mast


3. On Remembrance Day, the national flag 
of Canada shall be flown at half-mast for the 
entire day on federal premises throughout 
Canada.
