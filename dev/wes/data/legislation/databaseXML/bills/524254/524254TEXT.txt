





2nd Session, 37th Parliament,
51 Elizabeth II, 2002 










House of Commons of Canada










BILL C-251









An Act to amend the Patent Act (patented 
medicines)







R.S., c. P-4


Her Majesty, by and with the advice and 
consent of the Senate and House of Commons 
of Canada, enacts as follows:









1. Subsection 55.2(4) of the Patent Act is 
repealed.










2. For greater certainty, any obligation 
imposed on the Minister or a person under 
the Patented Medicines (Notice of 
Compliance) Regulations, registered as 
SOR/93-133, ceases to have effect on the 
coming into force of this Act.










3. This Act comes into force three months 
after the day on which it receives royal 
assent.
