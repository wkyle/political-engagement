





1st Session, 36th Parliament,
46 Elizabeth II, 1997










The House of Commons of Canada










BILL C-232









An Act to facilitate participation in the reserve 
force










Her Majesty, by and with the advice and 
consent of the Senate and House of Commons 
of Canada, enacts as follows:









SHORT TITLE






Short title


1. This Act may be cited as the Reserve 
Force Act.










LEAVE OF ABSENCE






Leave of 
absence


2. (1) Every employee of the public service 
of Canada or of a federal Crown corporation 
may, subject to any terms and conditions 
established by the Governor in Council by 
regulation, take an annual leave of absence 
from the employee's employment for a period 
not exceeding two months for the purpose of 
undergoing training or drill or serving in the 
reserve force.







Leave of 
absence


(2) The Minister of National Defence may 
enter into an agreement with any employer, 
other than an employer referred to in 
subsection (1), for the purpose of allowing an 
employee of the employer to take a leave of 
absence from the employee's employment for 
a period not exceeding two months for any of 
the purposes described in subsection (1).







Definition of 
``employee''


(3) In this section, ``employee'' means an 
employee who is a member of the reserve 
force.










REGULATIONS






Regulations


3. The Governor in Council may make 
regulations establishing terms and conditions 
for the purposes of subsection 2(1).
