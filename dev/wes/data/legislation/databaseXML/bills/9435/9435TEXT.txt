





1st Session, 36th Parliament,
46-47 Elizabeth II, 1997-98










The House of Commons of Canada










BILL C-449









An Act to amend the Employment Insurance 
Act (parental benefits)







1996, c. 23; 
1997, c. 26


Her Majesty, by and with the advice and 
consent of the Senate and House of Commons 
of Canada, enacts as follows:









1. Subsections 12(3) to (7) of the 
Employment Insurance Act are replaced by 
the following:







Maximum - 
special 
benefits


(3) Subject to subsection (7), the maximum 
number of weeks for which benefits may be 
paid in a benefit period










(a) because of pregnancy is 15;









(b) because the claimant is caring for one or 
more new-born children of the claimant or 
one or more children placed with the 
claimant for the purpose of adoption is 37 ; 
and









(c) because of a prescribed illness, injury or 
quarantine is 15.






Maximum - 
special 
benefits


(4) The maximum number of weeks for 
which benefits may be paid










(a) for a single pregnancy is 15; and









(b) for the care of one or more new-born or 
adopted children as a result of a single 
pregnancy or placement is 37 .






Combined 
weeks of 
benefits


(5) In a claimant's benefit period, the 
claimant may combine weeks of benefits to 
which the claimant is entitled because of a 
reason mentioned in subsection (3), but the 
maximum number of combined weeks is










(a) for the reasons mentioned in paragraphs 
(a) and (c), 30;









(b) for the reasons mentioned in paragraphs 
(b) and (c), 37;









(c) for the reasons mentioned in paragraphs 
(a) and (b), 52; and









(d) for the reasons mentioned in paragraphs 
(a), (b) and (c), 52.  






Combined 
weeks of 
benefits


(6) In a claimant's benefit period, the 
claimant may combine weeks of benefits to 
which the claimant is entitled because of a 
reason mentioned in subsections (2) and (3), 
but if the claimant is entitled under subsection 
(2)










(a) to 30 or fewer weeks of benefits, the 
claimant may, subject to the applicable 
maximums, receive a greater number of 
weeks of benefits if the claimant is also 
entitled to benefits for a combination of 
reasons mentioned in paragraph (5)(a), (b), 
(c) or (d);









(b) to more than 30 but fewer than 37 weeks 
of benefits, the claimant may, subject to the 
applicable maximums, receive a greater 
number of weeks of benefits if the claimant 
is also entitled to benefits for a combination 
of reasons mentioned in paragraph (5)(b), 
(c) or (d); and









(c) to 37 or more weeks of benefits, the 
claimant may, subject to the applicable 
maximums, receive a greater number of 
weeks of benefits if the claimant is also 
entitled to benefits for a combination of 
reasons mentioned in paragraph (5)(c) or 
(d).
