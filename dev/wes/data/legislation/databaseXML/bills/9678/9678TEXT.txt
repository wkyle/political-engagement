Her Majesty, by and with the advice and 
consent of the Senate and House of Commons 
of Canada, enacts as follows:









1. The Criminal Code is amended by 
adding the following after section 56:







Desecrating 
Canadian flag


56.1 (1) Every one who, without lawful 
excuse, wilfully burns, defaces, defiles, 
mutilates, tramples upon or otherwise 
desecrates the national flag of Canada is guilty 
of an offence punishable on summary 
conviction and liable to a fine not exceeding 
five thousand dollars or to imprisonment for a 
term not exceeding six months or to both.







Exception


(2) No person contravenes subsection (1) 
where the person disposes of the national flag 
of Canada because the flag has become worn, 
soiled or damaged.
