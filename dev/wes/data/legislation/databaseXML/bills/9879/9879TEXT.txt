





2nd Session, 36th Parliament,
48-49 Elizabeth II, 1999-2000










The House of Commons of Canada










BILL C-496









An Act to amend the Holidays Act (Christmas 
Day)







R.S., c. H-5


Her Majesty, by and with the advice and 
consent of the Senate and House of Commons 
of Canada, enacts as follows:









1. The Holidays Act is amended by adding 
the following after section 4:










CHRISTMAS DAY






Christmas 
Day


5. December 25 is a legal holiday and shall 
be kept as such throughout Canada under the 
name of ``Christmas Day''.
