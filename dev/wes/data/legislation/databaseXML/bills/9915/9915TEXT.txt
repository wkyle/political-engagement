






48-49 ELIZABETH II









CHAPTER 13











An Act to amend the National Defence Act 
(non-deployment of persons under the 
age of eighteen years to theatres of 
hostilities)










[Assented to 29th June, 2000] 







R.S., c. N-5


Her Majesty, by and with the advice and 
consent of the Senate and House of Commons 
of Canada, enacts as follows:









1. The National Defence Act is amended 
by adding the following after section 33:










Limitation on Deployment






Persons under 
eighteen


34. A person who is under the age of 
eighteen years may not be deployed by the 
Canadian Forces to a theatre of hostilities.
